True4k – Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between a device connection and a stream on True4k?
A device connection is how many devices you set up with your True4k account. A stream is how many devices can watch at the same time. If you configure 3 devices but have 1 stream, only one device can play live TV or VOD at once. To watch on multiple screens simultaneously, choose a plan with more devices/streams.
Why does the True4k order pop-up ask for my name and email before payment?
This step helps prevent mistakes (like typing the wrong email at checkout). By collecting your name and email first, True4k ensures your activation details and support info arrive in the correct inbox.

How does True4k reduce buffering?

What exactly is IPTV?
IPTV stands for Internet Protocol Television. Instead of satellite or classic cable, channels are delivered through the internet. True4k uses IPTV technology so you can watch on many devices using your connection.
Which devices are compatible with True4k?
True4k works on Smart TVs (with compatible apps), Android TV/Google TV, Fire TV/Firestick, Android phones and tablets, iOS devices (with IPTV apps), PCs (VLC/Kodi), and many other IPTV-ready devices.
